BABA AND HIS DOG

‘There lived a boy called Baba and his dog, snoopy Baba was a very stubborn boy who caused his parents a lot of trouble. Sometimes he fought and returned home with turned clothes. His parents had warned him several times but he would. One day, he got into trouble on his way to school, when he ordered his dog that went everywhere with him to bite a little boy who refused to give him his bicycle. Unknown to Babu, the boy’s father was a policeman who arrested him and his dog and took them to the police station. When his parents heard, they came begging for his release, telling the policeman how poor they were. But the policeman refused until his parents not knowing what else to do, went to a money lender to borrow money which was used to take the boy to the hospital to treat the dog bite.
